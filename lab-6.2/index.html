Bar Chart with Mouse Over and Sort

D3 Interactive Sorting Chart Demo

Mouse Over Effects: Hover over any bar to see it highlight in orange and display a custom SVG tooltip showing the exact value.

Add/Remove: Use the buttons to dynamically add or remove bars - all new bars will also have the interactive mouse over effects.

Sort: Click the Sort button to toggle between ascending and descending order. Watch the bars smoothly animate to their new positions!

This demonstrates D3's powerful sorting and transition capabilities for creating engaging, interactive data visualizations!
